Act as a professional web designer and UX/UI expert. Design a modern, clean, and dynamic website for a tax consultancy firm.

The website should reflect trust, professionalism, and financial expertise while maintaining a visually engaging and user-friendly experience.

Include the following sections and features:

1. Hero Section:

* Strong headline (e.g., “Smart Tax Solutions for Individuals & Businesses”)
* Subheading highlighting expertise and reliability
* Clear CTA buttons like “Book Consultation” and “Get Started”
* Background with subtle financial-themed graphics or abstract patterns

2. About Section:

* Brief introduction of the firm
* Highlight years of experience, certifications, and expertise
* Professional tone with trust-building elements

3. Services Section:

* Corporate Tax Filing
* Personal Tax Planning
* GST/VAT Consulting
* Audit & Compliance
* Use icons and card-based layout for clarity

4. Why Choose Us:

* Bullet points with key strengths (accuracy, compliance, fast service, expert advisors)
* Add stats (e.g., “500+ Clients”, “10+ Years Experience”)

5. Testimonials:

* Client feedback with names and roles
* Clean slider or card format

6. Contact Section:

* Simple contact form (Name, Email, Message)
* Office location, phone number, and email
* CTA for scheduling consultation

7. Footer:

* Quick links
* Social media icons
* Legal info (privacy policy, terms)

Design Guidelines:

* Use a professional color palette (navy blue, white, gold or green accents)
* Clean typography with strong hierarchy
* Plenty of whitespace for clarity
* Subtle animations (hover effects, smooth scrolling)
* Fully responsive (mobile-first design)
* Use modern UI components (cards, grids, icons)

Tone:

* Professional, trustworthy, and approachable
* Avoid clutter, keep layout minimal but impactful

Optional Enhancements:

* Dashboard-style visuals or financial charts
* Sticky navigation bar
* Live chat or chatbot integration

Output:

* Provide layout structure, UI suggestions, and sample content for each section
* Suggest font pairings and color palette with hex codes
* Include UX improvements for conversion optimization
