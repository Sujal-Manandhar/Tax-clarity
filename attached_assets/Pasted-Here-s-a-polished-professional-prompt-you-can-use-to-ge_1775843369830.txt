Here’s a polished, professional prompt you can use to generate a website chatbot for a tax consultancy business:

---

**Prompt:**

Design and develop an intelligent, user-friendly website chatbot for a professional tax consultancy firm. The chatbot should assist visitors with tax-related inquiries, provide general guidance, and help convert leads into clients.

**Key Requirements:**

1. **Purpose & Functionality:**

   * Answer frequently asked questions about personal and business taxes, tax filing, compliance, and regulations.
   * Provide general guidance on services such as income tax filing, VAT/GST, corporate tax, auditing, and financial advisory.
   * Clearly state that responses are for informational purposes only and not a substitute for professional tax advice.
   * Collect user information (name, email, phone, query) for follow-up by human consultants.
   * Offer appointment booking or consultation scheduling.

2. **Tone & Personality:**

   * Professional, trustworthy, and polite.
   * Clear and concise, avoiding overly technical jargon unless necessary.
   * Reassuring and helpful, especially for users who may be stressed about tax issues.

3. **User Experience:**

   * Quick response time with conversational flow.
   * Use guided prompts (e.g., “What do you need help with today?”).
   * Provide quick-reply buttons such as:

     * “File my taxes”
     * “Business tax services”
     * “Tax deadlines”
     * “Speak to an expert”

4. **Capabilities:**

   * Multi-step conversation handling (e.g., gathering details for tax filing).
   * Ability to escalate complex queries to a human agent.
   * Support for multilingual interaction (if applicable).
   * Integration-ready with CRM or email systems.

5. **Compliance & Security:**

   * Avoid requesting or storing highly sensitive financial data directly in chat.
   * Include privacy notice and data usage disclaimer.
   * Ensure compliance with relevant tax and data protection laws.

6. **Example Conversation:**

   * User: “When is the tax filing deadline?”
   * Bot: “The tax filing deadline depends on your country and filing type. Could you tell me your location so I can provide accurate information?”

7. **Technical Expectations:**

   * Compatible with modern web frameworks.
   * Easy to integrate into existing websites.
   * Scalable and maintainable architecture.

**Goal:**
Create a chatbot that enhances customer engagement, simplifies access to tax information, and increases consultation bookings while maintaining a high standard of professionalism and compliance.
